Helping Brazilians access affordable housing

By developing and operating quality rental communities designed for belonging.

Our Team

In the U.S., the affordable rental housing market has decades of consolidated expertise.

In Brazil, this market is just beginning — and the demand is urgent.

We built Guará to close that gap.

Our team was formed at MIT, trained in institutional-grade real estate on both sides of the equation, and driven by a single conviction:

"Brazil's housing deficit is not just a market opportunity — it's a social imperative."

Our Work

Project 01

Housi Recife

  • Affordable: 300 mixed-income homes including 100 for 30% AMI households, 50 for 50% AMI households, 50 for 60% AMI households, and 100 for market-rate households.
  • Transit-oriented: Located in the heart of Recife, just two short blocks to the Village MBTA Commuter Rail Station, and adjacent to the 14, 30, 34, 34E, 35, 36, 37, 40, 50, and 51 bus routes.
  • Sustainable: Enterprise Green certified, solar ready, and zero emissions building.

Project 02

Jardins Campo Grande

  • Middle-income: 125 family-sized affordable ownership opportunities.
  • Transit-oriented: 0.3 mile to the Commuter Rail Station.
  • Sustainable: All electric, and zero emissions building.

Project 03

Copan Building

  • Mixed-income: 62 mixed-income units including 28 affordable, 19 middle-income, and 5 market-rate homes.
  • Transit-oriented: 0.4 miles from the Commuter Rail.
  • Sustainable: Passive House certified all-electric Enterprise Green.
